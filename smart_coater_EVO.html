Smartcoater EVO & UV-R

Smartcoater Evo is the roller coater designed for roller coating panels and flat furniture doors. It is available in a range of configurations: with fixed or self-leveling floating head, with automatic longitudinal and transverse adjustment, ideal for working with panels that are not perfectly calibrated.

Smartcoater EVO and UV-R are two types of coating systems produced by the Italian company, Tiflex. These systems are used in the printing industry for applying coatings to various substrates, including paper, cardboard, plastic films, and metal sheets
Smartcoater EVO :

This is a water-based coating system that uses a flexographic or gravure printing unit to apply a uniform coating to the substrate. The system is equipped with advanced controls for precise coating thickness and speed, as well as a drying unit for fast and efficient curing of the coating.

Smartcoater UV-R :

UV-R is used in a variety of high-end printing applications, including luxury packaging, labels, and decorative printing. It can be used to apply various coatings, including high gloss, tactile, and metallic finishes
and as well as functional coatings such as scratch-resistant and chemical-resistant coatings.
