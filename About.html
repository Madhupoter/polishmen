ABOUT
POLISHMEN

Polishmen is a leading company in the field of wooden items. At Polishmen, we believe in the beauty and durability of wooden items, which is why we are dedicated to providing the highest level of service to our clients. Whether it's a wooden floor, stairs, or furniture, we have the expertise to remodel it according to your specifications and needs.

We, at Polishmen, also decided to bring in the same wood conserving technology from Italy after selling Italian coatings for about 25 years, as we understood the needs of the Indian market.

We are glad to introduce ourselves as the Largest application Centre for wood coatings having 75000 sft application area in Bangalore, which is considered to be the largest full fledged wood coating application centre with the complete CEFLA licensing for the South Asian market.

We would be fully equipped with a complete line of UV coating, Automatic & Robotic spraying system & manual application system with 6 spray booths, along with Excimer coating by the end of June 2023. The motive of this unit is to cater and support Indian Wood furniture manufacturing unit and to cater top of the line wood finishes on par with Italy, to MSME and SME. We would be finishing about 700 doors every day from raw doors to finish doors as well.
